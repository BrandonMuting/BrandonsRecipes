Mushroom Paste

Time: 30 mins
Serves: 1/2 cup

Carbs: free
INGREDIENTS
  • 4 portabella mushroom cups
  • 1/2 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tbsp thyme
  • 1/3 tbsp salt
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. wash mushrooms thoroughly
  2. slice mushrooms and fry for 10 minutes
  3. blend the mushrooms into a fine powder
  4. let the powder settle before pouring it out
  5. mix in the salt, thyme, and pepper flakes

Chocolate Avocado Spread

Time: 15 mins
Serves: 5

Carbs: 75
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 5 tbsp maple syrup
  • 3 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground chia seeds
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. blend ingredients
  2. divide mixture into 5 containers
