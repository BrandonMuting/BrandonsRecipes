Banana Pancake

Time: 10 mins
Serves: 1

Carbs: 30
INGREDIENTS
  • 1/2 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • cinnamon
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. mash the banana
  2. add eggs and stir
  3. fry until brown
