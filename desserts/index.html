Chocolate Zucchini Muffins

Time: 45 mins
Serves: 8

Carbs: 75
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 1/2 cups zucchini shredded
  • 2 eggs whisked
  • 3/4 cups almond butter
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp almond flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • salt
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. preheat oven to 375F
  2. shred the zucchini using a food processor or cheese grater
  3. use paper towel to squeeze water out of zucchini
  4. mix the zucchini with other ingredients until they are combined
  5. pour the batter into muffin tray
  6. bake in the oven for 25-35 minutes (until toothpick comes out clean)

Apple Cinnamon Muffins

Time: 30 mins
Serves: 8

Carbs: 60
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 apple diced
  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking powder
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. preheat oven to 320F
  2. mix dry ingredients first before adding in wet ingredients
  3. fill 8 muffin cups with batter
  4. after baking for 35 minutes, use a toothpick to see if they are done
