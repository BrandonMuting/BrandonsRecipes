Kale Chips

Time: 30 mins
Serves: 3

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 bunch baby kale
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • salt and pepper
INSTRUCTIONS
  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. wash and dry kale
  3. toss leaves with olive oil until evenly coated
  4. add kale to sheet of parchment paper
  5. bake for about 10 minutes
